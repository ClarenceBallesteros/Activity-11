Bachelor in Applied Computing Technology

The BACT is a for year course degree program that focuses on the practical aspects of the science of computing. The Program develops core skills in multimedia technology, web mobile development and network/system administration.Furthermore, it prepares learners with supervisory and managemnet skills. The curiculum from the BS Industrial Technology major in Computer Technology with CHED Certificate of proram Compliance (COPC) No. 31, series of 2021.

Program Objectives:

The Program prepares learners with the practical skills in multimedia technology, web/mobile development, and network system administration, and supervisorial skills to manage an organization in these areas of comoentencies, engage in a startup business of pursue a teaching career.

Specific Objectives:

The learner should be able:
  1. Demonstrate understanding of the importance of being ethical computing professional when making work-related decisions
  2. Develop products or processes by applying their knowledge in computer or network systems, development of productivity tools.
  3. Participate as an active member of a development/service team with leadership roles when appropriate
  4. Apply communication skills effectively to promote ideas, goals, processes or products.
  5. Make positive contributions to the community with the skills and abilities learned in the Program.
  6. Pursue lifelong for personal and professional growth through varied experiential and field-based opportunities
